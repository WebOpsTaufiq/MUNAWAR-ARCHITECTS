About Us

Led by a Civil Engineer & Urban Planner

Munawar Architect's is a full-spectrum architecture and construction studio based in Mysuru. We combine technical expertise in civil engineering with deep knowledge of urban planning and traditional Vaastu principles to deliver homes and commercial spaces that are both beautiful and functional.

Led by Mrs. Noor Saba, who brings advanced qualifications in architecture and urban planning, the studio offers end-to-end solutions — from the first sketch to the final key handover, including raw material supply.

Qualification B.E. (Civil Engineering)
Specialisation Advanced Architecture
